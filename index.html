SVSU Astro Club

SVSU Astro Club invites students of all experience levels to explore the universe through hands-on observation, guided learning, and real nights under the stars. Whether you’re taking your first look at the Moon or capturing deep-sky images, we’re here to help you build skills, ask questions, and discover just how vast—and accessible—the night sky can be.

SVSU Astro Club showcases our members' astrophotography on the regular, follow our socials to see everyone's incredible shots!
